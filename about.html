KAI

Slow curation, modern soul — objects that honor the makers, the materials, and the moments they inhabit.

Born from stillness

KAI was founded in 2025 at the intersection of Kyoto craftsmanship and Brooklyn energy. The name KAI (開) means "opening" — a portal to a more intentional way of living. We believe that the objects around us carry energy, stories, and silence. Our collection is an invitation to slow down, to choose fewer but better things, and to honor the hands that shape them.

What started as a small studio collaboration with ceramicists and weavers has grown into a global community of minimalists, collectors, and design lovers. KAI doesn’t follow trends — we follow texture, weight, and timelessness. Every item in our shop is a dialogue between tradition and the future.

Ethos: Earth, ethics, edit

We partner exclusively with small-scale artisans and ethical producers who share our commitment to sustainability. From reclaimed wood to natural dyes, each piece is made with low environmental impact. Our packaging is 100% plastic-free, and we’re on a path to become carbon-aware. KAI is a proud member of 1% for the Planet — we donate a portion of every sale to land restoration initiatives.

But beyond materials, our ethos is one of radical care: care for the maker, the user, and the planet. We aim to create a circular future where objects are loved, repaired, and passed down.
